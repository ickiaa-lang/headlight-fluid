Mission: Maintain vital resource levels. Glide close to floating storage nodes to salvage cargo. Avoid impact velocities against local asteroids. Pink Stations appear at fixed, calculated sector coordinates and hold their own cargo and 3 patch bays of 8 slots each — independent of your ship's storage, and saved with your game. Each station also farms its own O₂/H₂O/Food/Fuel reservoir over time and tops up your vitals automatically while you're in range.
